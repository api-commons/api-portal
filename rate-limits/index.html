--- layout: page title: Rate Limits ---

Provide developers with a straight view of rate limiting. Let them know what they can expect, and provide details on how they can make mor requests, either by paying, certifying their applications, or other means.

  • Make sure and break down the rate limits into numbers they can understand:
  • 0-500 API calls per day - FREE
  • 500 to 10,000 API calls per day at $0.00500 USD per call
  • 10,000 or more API calls per day at $0.00100 USD per call

Make sure and connect the rate limits to the pricing, and partner tiers, providing as much transparency about the how and why of rate limits for the platform.
